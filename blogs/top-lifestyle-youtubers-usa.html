Casey Neistat

Often referred to as the best vlogger on YouTube, Casey Neistat hails from NYC and began his journey a decade ago in February 2010. Since then he has gained a huge following of 12 million subscribers and accumulated 2.8 billion channel views from a total of 1030 videos as of April 2020. He shares videos of his experiences of trying out and testing new products and services. He was named “New Media Star- Man of the year” by GQ in 2016. His business ventures include, Beme App, which was later acquired by CNN. He also found a company called ‘368’, a creative space for creators to collaborate and inspire each other.

Nicole Guerriero

Born in Tampa, Florida, Nicole is a beauty and lifestyle vlogger. She started her journey on YouTube in 2006 and since then has gained 2.85 subscribers and her channel has received over 349 million views from 694 videos as of April 2020. She shares with her followers; intriguing looks for every occasion as well as daily vlogs. She was named Ryan Seacrest Best Beauty Guru of 2013. She runs her own blog called Naturally Nicole wherein she shares tutorials, product reviews, and fashion tips.

Tess Christine

Hailing from Wisconsin, Tess is a beauty and lifestyle vlogger who began her YouTube journey in 2011 by sharing videos on make-up hacks, hairstyle tutorials and fashion. Over the years, her channel has amassed 2.39 million subscribers and 199 million channel views from a total of 423 videos, as of April 2020. She is known for creating looks from famous television series and movies. From a few years, Tess has stated sharing videos on her personal workouts, diets, haul videos and travel vlogs as well. Her videos often feature her husband, Patrick.

Dulce Candy

An author, a war veteran, a content creator and mother to two kids, Dulce Candy is a YouTube star who has done it all. She was born in Mexico and raised in Southern California, USA. Starting her YouTube career in June 2008, Dulce has since gained 2.19 million subscribers and generated 329 million channel views from 1077 videos as of April 2020. She shares fun make up tutorials, fashion advice as well as some really personal moments. She hopes to inspire young women through her videos.

Aspyn and Parker

From Utah, Aspyn Ovard and Parker Ferris together started a YouTube channel called ‘Aspyn and Parker’ in 2011. Since then they have gained a huge following of 2.12 million followers and have received whopping 372 million channel views from 816 videos as of April 2020. They share videos providing a deep insight into their lives as a young and new family, sharing their celebrations, vacations, home décor etc. They have also been nominated for the prestigious “Vloggers of the year” award at the Shorty Awards. They also have their separate YouTube channels.

Sydney Serena

Sydney Parupsky, better known by her YouTube channel name, Sydney Serena, is a 19-year-old lifestyle and beauty vlogger from Minnesota, USA. She started her YouTube journey in July 2014 and since has managed to gather a following of 2.04 million subscribers and accumulated 294 million channel views. Her channel was previously known as BeautyBySyd. Uploading once every week, Sydney shares videos on her shopping sprees, haul and review videos and videos on recreating iconic celebrity looks.

Hailey Sani

Hailey Sani was born in Turkey and raised in the USA. She is a 19-year-old YouTuber whose channel was formerly known as ‘berrypinklips’. She began her YouTube career in April 2012 and has since then gathered 1.33 million subscribers and has 98 million channel views from 246 videos. Uploading every Friday, she shares videos on literally anything and everything from beauty and make up tutorials to fashion and college life and hence she does not like to categorize herself in any particular niche.
